Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a matatu?

Go to the Track page and allow location access. The app will show you matatus near your location.

Are the fare estimates accurate?

Our fares are provided by SACCOs and updated regularly. During peak hours, fares may vary slightly.

How do I report a bad driver?

After your trip, you can rate and review the driver on the Reviews page.
